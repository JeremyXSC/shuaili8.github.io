Biography

I am a third-year PhD student in the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Kwong-Sak Leung. I obtained my bachelor degree in Mathematics from Zhejiang University and my master degree in Mathematics from University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interest lies on online learning.
