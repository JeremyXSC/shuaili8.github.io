Shuai Li

Ph.D. Candidate

I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Chinese University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Kwong-Sak Leung. I obtained my bachelor degree in Mathematics from Zhejiang University and my master degree in Mathematics from University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interest lies on online learning, multi-armed bandits, statistical learning and online learning to rank.

I am expected to graduate in mid 2019 and open to full-time positions.

Email: shuaili@cse.cuhk.edu.hk

News

  • 02/2019: We have a new paper of Improved Algorithm on Online Clustering of Bandits, which generalizes to allow users with different appearing probabilities and uses sets to efficiently split and merge user clusters. Another paper is the first work on Stochastic Online Learning with Probabilistic Graph Feedback, which considers both one-step and cascade feedback.
  • 10/2018: It is my honor to receive Google PhD Fellowship 2018 in the field of machine learning.
  • 10/2018: Tor, Csaba and I have a new paper on Online Learning to Rank with Features, which extends the existing setting for general click models to deal with a large number of items and the expected reward is a linear function on item feature vectors. We bring up a general click model with only a few assumptions and design a simple but efficient algorithm.
  • 2018.4.30 - 2018.8.31: I am a research intern at DeepMind working together with Csaba Szepesvari and Tor Lattimore on the problem of online learning to rank.
  • 2017.6.5 - 2017.11.10: I am a research intern at Adobe Research working together with Branislav Kveton on the problem of offline evaluation. The work is accepted at KDD 2018.
