Shuai Li

Ph.D. Candidate

I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Chinese University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Kwong-Sak Leung. I obtained my bachelor degree in Mathematics from Zhejiang University and my master degree in Mathematics from University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interest lies on online learning, multi-armed bandits, statistical learning and online learning to rank. I am expected to graduate in mid 2019 and open to full-time positions.
